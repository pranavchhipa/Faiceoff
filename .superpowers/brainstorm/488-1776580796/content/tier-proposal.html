Why this structure? (research notes)

  • AspireIQ / Grin: creator tiers priced by usage scope — "organic only" vs "paid media rights" is the biggest lever
  • Shutterstock Custom / Adobe Stock Enhanced: standard license vs extended license = 4-8x price, mostly due to edit/derivative rights + commercial scope
  • AI UGC (Arcads, Icon.me, HeyGen): starter = limited variants no raw, premium = raw files + commercial rights
  • Indian talent licensing (actors/influencers): "campaign rights" (paid ads, 6-12 mo) typically 2.5-3x "social-only" rights — lines up with our 2.5x price ratio
  • The edit rights + re-approval loop is the smart part — it gives brand creative control (real pain point) while keeping creator's likeness protected (DPDP Act legal requirement). No platform in India does this cleanly yet.
